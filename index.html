Frequently Asked Questions

What is a home inspection?

A home inspection is a non-invasive, visual examination of a property's physical structure and systems. It's a professional assessment to identify major defects or safety concerns.

How long does an inspection take?

The time varies based on the size and age of the home, but a typical inspection for a standard-sized home takes between 2 to 3 hours.

Should I attend the inspection?

We strongly encourage you to be present. It's an excellent opportunity to ask questions, understand the home's systems, and see any issues firsthand.
